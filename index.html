More Cupcakes Galore is a premiere cupcake shop with no relationship to "Cupcakes Galore". We were established in Portland, OR back in 1920 and have been serving you the most scrumptuous cupcakes in the West ever since.

Our cupcakes our made with the finest ingredients that we obtain from local artisanal farms. We bake fresh cupcakes every day from 5am until noon.
